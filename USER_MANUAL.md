# Research Internship Agent - User Manual

Welcome to the **Research Internship Agent**, your premium AI-powered platform for discovering research opportunities and managing personalized outreach to professors worldwide.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### 1. Accessing the Dashboard
When you first log in, you'll be greeted by the **Dashboard**. This is your mission control where you can see:
- **Outreach Overview**: A visual chart of your activity over time.
- **Stats Grid**: Real-time tracking of discovered professors, pending drafts, and sent emails.
- **Top Research Areas**: AI-identified fields where your profile has the highest fit score.
- **AI Suggestion**: Personalized recommendations on professors or papers to check out.

---

## 🔍 Step 1: Discovering Professors

The **Discovery** module is where you find your potential mentors.

1. **Navigate to "Discovery"**: Click the "Discover" link in the sidebar.
2. **Search and Filter**:
   - Use the search bar to find specific universities (e.g., "Stanford", "TU Munich").
   - Filter by **Research Area** (e.g., Computer Vision, NLP).
   - Filter by **Country** to narrow down geographic preferences.
3. **Analyze Profiles**:
   - Click on a professor's name to view their full profile.
   - The AI will automatically calculate a **Fit Score** (0-100%) based on your background and their recent publications.
4. **Save for Outreach**:
   - If a professor matches your interests, click the **"Save to Drafts"** button. This will trigger the AI to generate a personalized email draft based on their latest research.

---

## 📝 Step 2: Managing Email Drafts

Once you've discovered professors, your personalized emails will appear in the **Drafts** section.

1. **Review Drafts**: Navigate to the "Drafts" page to see all generated emails.
2. **Status Tracking**:
   - `Pending`: Newly generated by AI.
   - `Edited`: Drafts you have manually tweaked.
   - `Ready`: Finalized drafts waiting to be sent.
3. **Edit and Personalize**:
   - Click the **Edit** icon to open the email editor.
   - While the AI handles the heavy lifting, adding a personal touch to the "Subject" or "Body" can significantly increase response rates.
4. **Delete**: If you decide not to contact a professor, use the **Delete** icon to remove the draft.

---

## 📧 Step 3: Sending Emails

Connecting your Gmail account allows you to send emails directly from the platform.

1. **Connect Gmail**: Go to **Settings** and click "Connect Gmail Account". Follow the Google OAuth prompts.
2. **Send Drafts**:
   - Once connected, a "Send" button will appear next to your `Ready` drafts.
   - You can also select multiple drafts and use **Bulk Actions** to send them all at once.
3. **Tracking**: Once sent, the status will change to `Sent`, and you can track replies in your actual Gmail inbox.

---

## 📊 Step 4: Tracking Analytics

The **Analytics** page provides deep insights into your outreach performance.

- **Response Rates**: See which research areas or universities are responding most frequently.
- **Fit Score Distribution**: Analyze the quality of your leads.
- **Timeline**: View your outreach consistency over weeks or months.

---

## 🛠️ Troubleshooting & Tips

- **Fit Score is Low**: Ensure your profile/resume details are up to date in the settings. The AI uses this data to compare against professor publications.
- **API Errors**: If you see a notification error, check your internet connection or ensure the backend service is running.
- **Draft Quality**: For the best results, provide the AI with specific papers you've read in the "Experience" section of your settings.

---

## 🛡️ Security & Privacy

- **Data Safety**: Your email drafts and discovered leads are stored securely.
- **Gmail Access**: The platform only requests "Compose" and "Send" permissions. We do not read your personal emails.
